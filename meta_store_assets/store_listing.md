## Short Description
Experience your favorite PlayStation 4 and PlayStation 5 games on massive virtual screens in your Meta Quest headset with low-latency remote play.

## Long Description

Turn your Meta Quest into the ultimate gaming display! **Horizon Stream** is a dedicated, open-source PlayStation Remote Play client built specifically for the Meta Quest ecosystem. 

Whether you want to play fast-paced shooters on a massive, theater-sized virtual screen or lay back and enjoy immersive RPGs while staying aware of your surroundings in mixed reality, this app delivers a seamless and highly optimized remote play experience straight from your PS4 or PS5.

### Key Features:
* **High-Performance Streaming:** Enjoy crisp, low-latency gameplay at 1080p (and HDR 4K on supported PS5 connections) with buttery-smooth 60fps frame rates.
* **Immersive Virtual Displays:** Resize, reposition, and curve your screen anywhere in your physical space using Meta Quest's seamless window management.
* **Native Controller Support:** Connect your DualSense or DualShock 4 controller directly to your headset via Bluetooth for precise, zero-compromise input.
* **Open Source:** Fully transparent, ad-free, and community-driven. Horizon Stream complies with the AGPLv3 license and is built upon the renowned `chiaki-ng` project.
